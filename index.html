Overlanding

Overlanding can also be called motorized camping, the difference is that overlanders explore areas that are new to them. I.e., If you are visiting Okanogan-Wenatchee National Forest, planning out your new routes, and your campsites (In most National Forests you can camp anywhere as long as you follow the marked guidelines to do so). You will discover the most epic views you have ever seen!

Backpacking

Not beautiful place can be driven to, sometimes we have to walk to find those hidden gems. Backpacking will get you to those gems, and stay the night to enjoy it even longer.

Canoe/Kayak Camping

What if that spot I want to see is surrounded by water? Well, canoe/kayak camping is your option! Many of those small islands you see in a river or lake can be rowed to, and camping is allowed. We will help you Find them!
